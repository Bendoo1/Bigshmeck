About Me

I am an aspiring electronic artist with major influences from Aphex Twin and Bonobo. I have been looking to expand my horizons by using as many elements of house, rock and dance music as I can in each song. Each of my songs is also very much a personal experience in my life personified into music. I look forward to moving away from electronic music in future and using more natural elements in my work. I have not played any gigs as of yet and am hoping to start gigging as soon as I can figure out how to make my music playable in a live setting. I also love 80's music a great deal and borrow a lot from that influence too.

I have other interests in programming, the internet and sound design. I hope these will all come in handy when progressing in my career. In my programming I am not only interested in web design but also in machine programming, particularly in physics, robotics and studies of audio/waves.
